Newserchhip North VC

Village Council Field Data App for property tax collection and census

Open the App

What is this?

This is the official data collection tool for the Newserchhip North Village Council. Field workers use it to record property details, capture GPS locations and photos, and log household information — all stored securely and accessible to administrators for reporting and export.

Features

GPS photo capture & timestamp

GPS + Photo Capture

Every property gets a GPS-stamped photo taken on-site

Offline

Works Offline

Collect data in the field with no signal — syncs when back online

Ranges

Sticker Ranges

Assign numbered sticker ranges to field workers for organised coverage

Households & census

Household Census

Record family members, ages, and genders for population statistics

CSV & ZIP export

Export & Reports

Export property records as CSV, members as CSV, or photos as ZIP

Installable

Install as App

Install the PWA on any phone or tablet for a native app experience

Access

Only registered field workers and supervisors can use the data collection app. If you are a field worker, your supervisor will provide your login credentials. To request access or report an issue, contact the Village Council administrator.
